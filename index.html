Patflix

This website showcases full CRUD capabilities, a simple, responsive replication of a popular streaming service's website, and some of the films I have made with my friends over the years.

Made with HTML, CSS, Javascript, EJS, Express, Mongo, and AtlasDB.

Lead Instructor at iD Tech

During the summer of 2019, I was lucky to work at iD Tech, one of the lead instructors at the Harvard campus. I taught topics such as Javascript, 3D modelling, and virtual reality with C# and Unity, in addition to leading a team of instructors to give a group of kids the best summer possible.

Lightning Mafia

Have you ever played the game Mafia, or Werewolf? You need to use your specialized roles to figure out who the villains are in your group, and then convince your teammates to vote for them! Fully playable with other people online, creating a room for you and your friends where the game keeps track of roles as they change through the night.

Made with Meteor, utilizing HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, React, and MongoDB

Researcher at Emerson College FACE Lab

During the parts of the year that I attend Emerson College, I assist Professor Ruth Grossman in her studies pertaining to communication science and disorders. I write computer programs that convert motion capture data into a more usable format, as well as edit videos in Adobe Premiere.

Pokemon or Medication?

A website that presents you with a randomized Pokemon and Medication combo, and you have to guess which is which, along with alternate modes, registration for saving scores, and a leaderboard.

Made using Laravel with HTML, CSS, PHP, Vue, and MySql.
