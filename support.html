Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at contact@digitalbouquet.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and the app version. Screenshots are helpful!

How do I request a feature?

We love hearing your ideas! Send us an email at contact@digitalbouquet.com with your suggestion.

How do I restore my purchases?

In most of our apps, you can restore purchases from the settings menu. If you're having trouble, please contact us and we'll help you out.
